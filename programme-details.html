NSAR Workshop 2026

Programe Details

Venue : NSUT (Main Campus), Sector-3 Dwarka, New Delhi-110078
Eligibility
The national workshop is open to faculty, scientists, students and research scholars.
Certificate
Certificates shall be issued to all the participants.
Resource persons
Eminent scientist and specialists on topics related to nuclear chemistry, nuclear physics, nuclear reactor, production and application of radioisotopes, nuclear analytical techniques and Health and safety aspects will be the resource persons of the workshop.
Laboratory sessions
The lectures will be supplemented by hands-on laboratory experiments for the benefit of participants.
BRNS session
In addition, special session will also be held on research funding opportunities by the BRNS for the benefit of participants associated with teaching and research.
For any query, contact: nsarworkshop2026@gmail.com
